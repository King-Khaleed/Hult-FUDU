# **App Name**: FUDU Hult Hub

## Core Features:

- Landing Page Overview: Provide a clear and concise overview of the Hult Prize competition, eligibility, and benefits.
- Registration Form: Allow students to register for the Hult Prize FUDU 2026 competition through a form.
- Timeline Display: Visually showcase the important dates and deadlines related to the Hult Prize FUDU 2026 competition.
- FAQ Section: Provide answers to common questions about the competition, rules, and registration process.
- Social Proof Display: Display testimonials and success stories from past participants. Showcase visual badges and/or the logos of successful projects from your school to attract ambitious students.
- WhatsApp Integration: Integrate a WhatsApp link or button for direct communication and support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light Pink (#FFB6C1) to establish a friendly and approachable tone.
- Background color: Off-White (#FAF9F6), to create a soft and clean aesthetic and ensures high readability.
- Accent color: Lavender (#E6E6FA), to complement the pink scheme and add a touch of elegance.
- Body and headline font: 'Nunito', a sans-serif font to provide a modern, accessible, and easily readable look. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use clean and modern icons for key sections, e.g., a calendar icon for the timeline, a question mark icon for the FAQ section, etc.
- Single-page scrollable layout with clearly defined sections, each focusing on a specific aspect of the Hult Prize competition.
- Subtle animations on scroll to reveal content sections, ensuring an engaging and dynamic user experience.
